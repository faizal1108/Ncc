About NCC

Established in 1948, the National Cadet Corps (NCC) is the largest uniformed youth organization in the world, dedicated to fostering qualities of character, comradeship, discipline, and selfless service among young citizens and committed to the Nation's Second Line of Defence.

Operating under the Ministry of Defence, the NCC provides students with fundamental military training and installs a strong sense of patriotism and national integration, adhering strictly to its inspiring motto: "Unity and Discipline".

Our Contribution

NCC in KI

Kumaraguru Institutions takes immense pride in hosting robust and exceptionally active National Cadet Corps (NCC) units. The institution strongly supports the cadets' patriotism, commitment, and dedication to being part of the nation's Second Line of Defence.
